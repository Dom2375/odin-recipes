Brownies

Description

A foolproof brownie recipe

Brownie Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoas powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Frosting Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter.
  3. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  4. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
  7. To Make Frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
